Finla App Prototype

At Aparajayah Technologies, we are have developed the prototype for the Finla eCommerce App, a groundbreaking platform designed to revolutionize the online shopping experience. Finla is known for offering high-quality, essential products, and we have ensured the app is crafted to provide flawless navigation, security, and customer satisfaction.

Key Features of the Finla eCommerce App:

  • Coin-Based Purchasing System: Finla introduces an innovative pre-order feature using Finla coins, where users can top up their Finla Coins to secure upcoming products. Each coin is valued at 1 rupee, making it an easy and engaging way to shop.
  • Exclusive Discounts on Pre-Orders: With our coin-based system, users can enjoy a 30% discount on pre-orders, offering exceptional savings credited directly to their Finla Coin dashboard.
  • Cash-on-Delivery and EMI Options: To ensure a smooth and flexible shopping experience, Finla offers both cash-on-delivery and easy EMI plans with partnered banks.
  • Superfast Free Delivery: Orders placed using Finla Coins come with superfast, free delivery, giving customers added convenience.
  • Upcoming Deals: Finla keeps users ahead with exclusive deals and offers, ensuring they never miss out on the best products.
At Aparajayah Technologies, we’ve implemented the latest technologies and features to ensure Finla’s app prototype provides a flawless and secure eCommerce experience, tailored for the Indian market.
